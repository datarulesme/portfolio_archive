This graphic was paired with an article about the early release of non-violent drug-offenders from federal custody in October 2016. The article took a micro approach, telling the stories of select individuals. The graphic below's purpose was to put those individuals identities in context and answer the question: Who are the 13,000+ other prisoners who qualified for early release?
