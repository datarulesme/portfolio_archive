This interactive was part of collaboration with the Bay area investigative team on a story about a San Francisco community-college chancellor who was using public money to purchase alcohol and dine at pricey restaurants. It functions as a tool, allowing readers to explore the data behind this claim and compare his spending to those in other school districts.
